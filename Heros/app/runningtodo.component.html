Inspired by completing my first marathon and cycling from LA to San Franciso in 2016, I have decided on my 2017 sporting goal: Complete 12 marathon events*. I've decided to do this as an excuse to visit parts of the world I otherwise wouldn't, whilst building upon the fitness I achieved in 2016. My first marathon was painful, exhausting and nausiating but so much fun all at the same time. This year will certainly be tough on my legs, but will be something I'll never forget. It may even be the start of a life long addiction.

*I'm currently counting Marathons, Triathlons and 100mile Bike Rides as a marathon event Complete Upcoming
